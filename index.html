Taylor Swift

The Queen that become the music industry

Here's a summary of Taylor's biggest records

  • 2006 - Became the youngest artist in history to have written and performed a No. 1 song on the Hot Country Songs chart with Our Song.


  • 2009 - Became the youngest person to ever win entertainer of the year at the Country Music Association Awards.


  • 2010 - Became the youngest artist ever to receive the Grammy for Album of the Year award with Fearless.


  • 2016 - Became the first woman to ever win twice the Grammy for Album of the Year award with 1989.


  • 2019 - Has won the most American Music Awards in history with a total 40 awards.


  • 2021 - Holds the record for most No. 1 hits on Billboard's US Digital Song Sales chart.


  • 2021 - Became the first woman to win the Grammy for the Album of the Year three times with Folklore.


  • 2022 - All Too Well (10 Minutes Version) is the longest song in history to reach No. 1.


  • 2022 - Following the release of Red (Taylor's Version), broke Shania Twain's record for the most weeks at No. 1 on the Billboard Top Country Albums Chart.


  • 2022 - Broke the record for most video of the year wins at the VMAs.


  • 2022 - Became the only artist in history to claim all top 10 entries on the Billboard Hot 100 in a single week.


  • 2023 - Broke her own record for the most-streamed album in a single day in Spotify history with the release of 1989 (Taylor's Version).


  • 2023 - Broke a Spotify record as the most-streamed artist in a single day on the platform.


  • 2024 - Has been nominated for Song of the Year more times than any other artist in Grammys history.


  • 2024 - Became the first artist to win the Grammy for Album of the Year four times.
